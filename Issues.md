Issues:

- It's the product of an old tool we no longer have access to. The
  following are currently nigh impossible:
    - Changing the menu bar.
    - Changing formatting.

- It doesn't work well with mobile devices.

- Even testing is a pain in the ass.

- The pages are a nightmare to edit.
  - They consist of HTML interspersed with formatting commands.
  - Directories often contain large, lumped-together sets of obsolete files. 

- The layout of the pages' directory structure is a chaotic mess.
  - The files for various category pages live in a directory _above_
    the grouped content, with a redirection-only index.html gluing the
    gap.
  - There are lots of probably-obsolete files, preserved out of a fear of deletion.

- Our current model doesn't have separate pages for events, so
  perusing past history is impossible.
