 Country Store

Note: Since the Fourth of July falls on the weekend, the July club meeting will take place on the second Sunday of the month, the 12th. The meeting will forego a mini-show, since well need the table space for the Country Store! If youve been to the event in the past, then you know that its one of the most entertaining of the season, and a great opportunity for you to share your bounty with your fellow members.



How does it work?

 It's a mix of potluck and raffle: attendees bring donations that they think other members would appreciate, and leave them at the appropriate table (see below). They then buy raffle tickets at 5 for $1, and deposit tickets at any table whose items interest them.

Once ticket-depositing time is over, volunteers at each of the four tables take turns drawing tickets from the pots, in small batches, and reading them aloud. If an attendees ticket number is called, they go to the corresponding table and grab their item of choice. We keep at this until were out of tickets, items, or time.


Details

  • As noted above, the tickets are sold in batches of 5 for $1: you decide how many you would like to buy.
  • The tickets are two-parters: you keep one, and put the other in a pot. Because the ticket numbers can be hard to hear, it's a good idea to mark the tickets you place in the pots. Some people mark their initals with Sharpies; others use personal stamps or stickers.
  • How you distribute your tickets is entirely up to you. You can spread them evenly between all four tables, leave them all at one table, or anything in between.
  • The table categories are:
    • Cacti
    • Succulents
    • Miscellaneous items (books, pots, rocks, etc.)
    • Desserts
  • If you do bring desserts, please make certain that they are individually wrapped.
  • If you have a loud voice, or at least one that projects well, please volunteer! Wed appreciate your help in calling out the numbers at each of the four tables.
  • Although we wont have a pot luck this month, well have water available.
  • Our mini show will resume next month, in August.
  • There will be no regular raffle this month, since the Country Store is itself a big raffle.

We hope youll join us for this annual event!
